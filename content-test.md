## School of Computer Science and Engineering  
**The Hebrew University of Jerusalem**

Welcome to the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. Our school is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation, and has been a leading academic center in Israel for decades.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to:

- Provide world-class education in computer science and engineering.
- Advance knowledge through impactful research.
- Train future leaders in academia, industry, and entrepreneurship.
- Contribute technological solutions to local and global challenges.

## Academic Programs

We offer a range of programs for students at different stages:

- **B.Sc. in Computer Science**
- **B.Sc. in Computer Science and Computational Biology**
- **M.Sc. in Computer Science**
- **Ph.D. in Computer Science**

Our curriculum combines strong theoretical foundations with practical skills in software development, data science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and more.

## Research Excellence

Faculty members and students at our school conduct cutting-edge research in areas such as:

- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Algorithms and Complexity
- Cybersecurity and Cryptography
- Data Science and Databases
- Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing
- Theoretical Computer Science

Our researchers collaborate with leading universities, research institutes, and technology companies in Israel and around the world.

## Community and Innovation

The school fosters a vibrant and inclusive academic community. Students benefit from:

- Modern labs and research facilities
- Hackathons, seminars, and industry events
- Internship and career opportunities with top tech companies
- Support for entrepreneurship and startup initiatives

## Contact

For more information, please visit the Hebrew University website or contact the School of Computer Science and Engineering office.
